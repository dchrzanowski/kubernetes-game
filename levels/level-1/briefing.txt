  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Investigator. You've been called to the scene.

  Aldric Systems. Abandoned 11 days ago. No sign of the
  engineers. One terminal was still on when we arrived.

  The frontend deployment is crash-looping. Whatever the
  engineers were working on when they disappeared — this
  was it. Find out why the pods died.

  Namespace: level-1

  The last known database connection string found on a
  sticky note near the terminal:
    postgres://db:5432/app

  You also find a partially burned out sticky notes that says:
    kubectl set env deployment/frontend ........ -n level-1

  Commands to begin your investigation:
    kubectl get pods -n level-1
    kubectl describe pod <pod-name> -n level-1
    kubectl logs <pod-name> -n level-1

  OBJECTIVE: Bring all pods in level-1 to Running state.
