  CASE FILE 003 — "The Swarm"
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Something is wrong with the api-server deployment.

  It bears the name of one of the missing engineers —
  we don't know if that's significant. What we do know
  is that it's running on a single pod, and the cluster
  logs show traffic volumes that should not be possible
  for a building with no one in it.

  The system is being overwhelmed. Scale the deployment
  to 3 replicas before it collapses entirely.

  Namespace: level-3

  Commands to begin your investigation:
    kubectl get deployment api-server -n level-3
    kubectl get pods -n level-3

  OBJECTIVE: api-server deployment must have at least 3 ready replicas.

  As you read the objective you turn around and read a huge writing on the wall (in blood) that says:
  kubectl scale deployment SRV_NAME HOW_DO_I_SET_REPLICAS__DASH_DASH_WHAT????
